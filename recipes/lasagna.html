Lasagna

Nothing beats classic lasagna. And this is the easiest recipe you will ever make!

Lasagna is filling, easy to assemble and make, highly adaptable and keeps well, making it an amazing choice for chefs creating menus and home cooks alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.

  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.

  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
