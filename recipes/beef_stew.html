Beef Stew

Delicious beef stew! The longer this simmers, the better!

This classic stick-to-your-ribs stew is the ideal project for a chilly weekend. Beef, onion, carrots and potatoes come together in cozy harmony. If you are feeding a crowd, good news: It doubles (or triples) beautifully.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cubed beef stew meat
  • 3 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • Â½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 4 stalks celery, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook beef in oil over medium heat until brown.
  2. Stir in rosemary, parsley and pepper.
  3. Add water and bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 hour.
  4. Stir potatoes, carrots, celery, and onion into the pot.
  5. Cover and simmer 1 hour more.
